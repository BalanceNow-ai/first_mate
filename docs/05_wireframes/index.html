Yes please — what does the 100-hour service involve for my F200?
You · Just now
For your Yamaha F200XCA at 100 hours, the service manual specifies the following:

1. Water Pump Impeller — This is the most critical item. Yamaha specifies replacement every 100 hours or annually. A failed impeller causes rapid overheating.
2. Engine Oil & Filter — 10W-30 4-stroke marine oil, 6.1 litres. Use Yamalube or equivalent FC-W certified oil.
3. Fuel Filter — Replace the VST (Vapour Separator Tank) filter.
4. Spark Plugs — NGK LKAR6A-9 (6 plugs). Check gap: 0.9mm.
5. Gear Oil — Lower unit gear oil, 460ml.
